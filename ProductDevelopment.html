Marketing and Sales Strategies for NES

Target Market Focus

Given the diverse target market (oatmeal-based porridge manufacturers, protein shake manufacturers, etc.), a multi-pronged marketing approach is essential.

Key Marketing Strategies

  • Educational Content Marketing: Create high-quality content to educate customers about omega-3 benefits.
  • Digital Marketing: Use SEO, PPC, and social media to engage with target audiences.
  • Public Relations: Build relationships with influencers and journalists to generate media coverage.
  • Trade Shows: Participate in events to network and showcase products.
  • Sales Force Development: Train a dedicated sales team to promote products effectively.

Sales Channels

  • Direct Sales: Build relationships with decision-makers in target companies.
  • Distribution Partnerships: Collaborate with established distributors.
  • Online Sales: Offer direct-to-consumer sales through e-commerce platforms.

Pricing Strategy

  • Value-Based Pricing: Position the product as a premium ingredient.
  • Flexible Pricing: Offer models based on customer volume and contract terms.
